BRIEF DISCRIPTION ABOUT THE APPLICATION
#In Sierra Leone, Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) represent the backbone of the 
domestic economy, employing a substantial segment of the population. However, a 
significant operational hurdle for these businesses is the lack of formalized, 
accessible, and automated financial tracking frameworks. Many micro-retailers and 
local market traders rely on physical ledger books, which are highly vulnerable to 
loss, damage, structural miscalculations, and geometric tracking errors. Without clean 
visibility into overhead expenses, discounts offered, and base unit margins, operators
face an inability to calculate true profit margins vs gross cash intake.#

An intuitive, modern GUI-based desktop application designed for small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Sierra Leone. 
This tool enables business owners to calculate total costs, revenues, and net profits seamlessly while keeping track of sales logs during a single session.

The application aligns with Sustainable Development Goal 8 (SDG 8): Decent Work and Economic Growth by providing a structured framework for data-driven decisions in local businesses.Technical Architecture OverviewThe system relies entirely on Python’s native GUI library, Tkinter, utilizing basic functional programming patterns and event listeners.
